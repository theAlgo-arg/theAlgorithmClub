Vata

वात · Air & Space

Governs movement, breathing, circulation, and nerve impulses. When balanced, brings creativity and vitality. When disturbed, causes anxiety and dryness.

Pitta

पित्त · Fire & Water

Governs digestion, metabolism, and transformation. When balanced, brings intelligence and courage. When disturbed, causes inflammation and anger.

Kapha

कफ · Earth & Water

Governs structure, lubrication, and immunity. When balanced, brings strength and compassion. When disturbed, causes lethargy and congestion.

Our Philosophy

Treating the root,
not the symptom

Ayurveda doesn't suppress illness — it restores balance. Every consultation begins with understanding your unique Prakriti, the innate constitution that shapes everything from your digestion to your dreams.

"Swasthaya swasthya rakshanam, aturasya vikara prashamanam cha."
— Charaka Samhita
I

Ahara — Nourishment

Food is your first medicine. Your Vaidya prescribes a diet aligned with your Dosha, season, and agni (digestive fire).

II

Vihara — Lifestyle

Dinacharya (daily routine), yoga, and pranayama tailored to restore your natural rhythms.

III

Aushadhi — Herbs

Classical formulations from Ashtanga Hridayam — Ashwagandha, Triphala, Brahmi, and hundreds more — prescribed precisely.

IV

Sadvritta — Conduct

Right conduct, relationships, and mental hygiene. Ayurveda is a complete science of life, not just physical medicine.
