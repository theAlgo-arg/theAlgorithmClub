About

Making the
invisible visible

I started my practice after a decade leading creative at studios in London and Amsterdam. What I found is that the most powerful brands aren't built on aesthetics — they're built on clarity of thought, expressed through precise visual decisions.

My work lives in the space between strategy and craft. I take on a small number of projects each year to ensure every one gets the attention it deserves.

How I work

The Process

I

Discovery

Deep dive into your business, audience, and competitive landscape. We define what success looks like before any design begins.

II

Strategy

Position, personality, and direction. A written creative strategy becomes the filter every design decision passes through.

III

Design

Iterative exploration in focused rounds. You're involved throughout — not presented a finished solution with nowhere to go.

IV

Delivery

Production-ready assets, documented guidelines, and onboarding for your team. The work should grow without me.
