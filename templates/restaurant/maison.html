The Philosophy

Food as a form of memory.

At Maison, the kitchen begins not with a recipe but with a question: what does this season want to say? Our chefs work with fewer than twelve farms, chosen for their commitment to heritage cultivation and flavor integrity.

The result is a constantly evolving tasting menu — never the same twice, always rooted in place and in time.
