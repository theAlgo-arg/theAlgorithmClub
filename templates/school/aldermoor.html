Life at Aldermoor

A campus that works
as hard as you do.

🏆 Times Higher Education University of the Year 2024

Life here runs well beyond the lecture hall. Aldermoor's 87-acre main campus sits 12 minutes from the city centre, with dedicated residential colleges, an Olympic-standard sports complex, and over 200 student societies.

01
Guaranteed first-year accommodation All undergraduate offer holders receive a guaranteed room in a residential college within 10 minutes walk of their faculty.
02
Wellbeing embedded, not bolted on Every student has a named personal tutor and access to counselling with no waiting list for first appointments.
03
Careers from year one Aldermoor's careers service is ranked first in the country for graduate employer connections and internship placement rates.

Your application.

We want to admit the right people, not the most coached applicants. Our process rewards genuine ability and potential, and we're transparent about every stage.

Choose your programme Use our programme finder to filter by subject, study mode, and intake year.

Submit your application Undergraduate via UCAS. Postgraduate directly through our applicant portal — no fee.

Interview or portfolio review Select programmes require an interview or portfolio. We offer remote and in-person slots from October.
