Policies & Privacy

Transparency, responsibility, and trust.

These policies explain how The Algorithm Club delivers services, handles third-party costs, protects client information, and defines the responsibilities of both parties.

01

Introduction

The Algorithm Club provides website development, maintenance, consulting, advertising support, and digital transformation services.

02

Definitions

  • Client refers to the purchaser of services.
  • Service Charges refer to payments made directly to The Algorithm Club.
  • Partnership Charges refer to third-party costs such as hosting, domains, cloud infrastructure, software licenses, and similar services.
03

Service Charges & Partnership Charges

  • Invoices may contain Service Charges and Partnership Charges.
  • Service Charges are retained by The Algorithm Club.
  • Partnership Charges are paid to external providers.
  • The exact Partnership Charges will be clearly displayed whenever applicable.
  • We attempt to minimize third-party costs whenever technically appropriate.
  • Free hosting solutions may be used when suitable.
04

Discounts & Promotions

  • Discounts generally apply only to Service Charges.
  • Partnership Charges may still remain payable.
  • A promotion advertised as 100% Off may reduce Service Charges to zero while third-party costs remain.
05

Ownership Rights

  • The client retains ownership of purchased domains.
  • The client retains ownership of hosting accounts purchased on their behalf.
  • The client retains ownership of all content supplied by them.
  • The Algorithm Club claims no ownership over client business assets.
06

Hosting & Infrastructure

  • Hosting providers usually charge in advance.
  • Hosting upgrades may not qualify for full refunds.
  • Migrations may involve additional costs.
  • Unused hosting periods may not be refundable.
  • Refund eligibility depends on provider policies.
07

Maintenance & Support

  • Some plans include a free support period.
  • After that period, additional work may require a maintenance extension.
  • Corrections for delivery mistakes caused by us will still be provided within reasonable limits.
08

Security Responsibilities

  • Reasonable security practices are followed.
  • We accept responsibility for obvious implementation mistakes caused by our work.
  • We do not guarantee protection against advanced attacks, zero-day vulnerabilities, DDoS attacks, or third-party breaches.
  • No digital system can be guaranteed completely secure.
09

Advertising & Growth Disclaimer

  • Websites and advertising campaigns do not guarantee business growth.
  • Revenue, traffic, rankings, and conversions cannot be guaranteed.
  • Results vary depending on factors beyond our control.
10

Testimonials & Portfolio Usage

  • Testimonials are only displayed with permission.
  • Case studies and portfolio examples are only shown with client approval.
  • Clients are never required to provide endorsements.
11

Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Cancellation and refund eligibility depends on project progress.
  • Refund eligibility depends on service type.
  • Refund eligibility depends on third-party commitments.
  • Partnership Charges may be non-refundable.
12

Privacy Policy

  • Client information is used only for service delivery.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • Information may be shared with providers only when necessary to deliver services.
  • Reasonable efforts are made to protect stored information.
13

Limitation of Liability

  • The Algorithm Club shall not be liable for indirect or consequential damages.
  • Total liability shall not exceed the amount paid directly to The Algorithm Club for the affected service.
14

Changes To These Terms

  • These terms may be updated periodically.
  • The latest published version shall apply to future transactions.
